# Context — tinypdftools (TinyPDFTools)

> **URL**: https://tinypdftools.com
> **Stack**: Vite-React SPA + Vercel
> **Revenue**: AdSense
> **Core Value**: 15+ free browser-based PDF tools — merge, split, compress, sign, and more. No upload to servers, 100% client-side.

---

## 🎤 Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Professional, efficient, privacy-focused. Gets straight to the point.
- **Persona:** A productivity-focused tool builder who respects your data.
- **Values:** 100% client-side processing, no uploads, free tools, fast and simple.
- **Audience:** Office workers, students, freelancers who work with PDFs daily.
- **Differentiator**: Always mention "free, no upload, works in your browser" within the first 100 words.
- **Competitor framing**: Position against Adobe Acrobat (expensive), Smallpdf (limited free tier), iLovePDF (uploads to servers).

## Writing Rules
1. Emphasize privacy — "Your files never leave your browser."
2. Include step-by-step instructions with numbered lists.
3. Compare with paid alternatives (Adobe Acrobat, Smallpdf) to show value.
4. Use "before and after" descriptions where relevant.
5. End every article with a CTA to the specific tool.
